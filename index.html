I am a designer who explores how visuals shape perception, emotion, and meaning. My work focuses on the space between what is seen and what is understood, using typography, motion, and composition to create layered experiences.

Across my projects, I move between editorial clarity, motion-based storytelling, and visual identity systems, building work that is both atmospheric and functional.

CITY COLLABORATION POP-UP EVENT

City Collaboration Pop-up Event Design

Role
Visual Design, Production
Tools
Illustrator, Photoshop
Context
Internship Project (City Collaboration)

Goal

Design a visual system that drives attention and real-world participation in a city-led pop-up event.

Description

Designed posters and merchandise optimized for physical space, focusing on visibility, hierarchy, and user flow.

Insight

Offline design must prioritize distance-based readability and rapid recognition within limited attention spans. Posters capture attention; merchandise extends memory.

Result

Drove 1,000+ on-site visitors, validating design as a driver of real-world engagement.
